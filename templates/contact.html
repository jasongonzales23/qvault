Serious about Relationships

We are a members-only matchmaking service. Q-VAULT is for gay and lesbian individuals in the Central Ohio area ready for commitment and self-discovery. We are not a hook-up site.

Serious about discretion and Privacy

We personally interview every member to ensure you only meet others who are serious about relationships like you. Profiles are not open to the public.

Serious about Our Community

Q-VAULT is based in Columbus, OH and we are committed to supporting our local community and giving back to pay it forward. A portion of our proceeds will be directed to Equality Ohio, which is a statewide non-profit agency working towards achieving full equality for all Ohioans.
